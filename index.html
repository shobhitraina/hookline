The problem

Your agency hands you a lead, then goes dark.

They run the ads, drop a lead in your inbox, and the trail goes cold. Nobody knows which creative actually worked, what the sales rep said on the call, or why one buyer converted and another didn't. And every agency now says "AI" without shipping a single thing your team can use.

The opaque agency

Monthly PDFs and vanity metrics. The sales call is a black box, no record, no context. Reps re-pitch leads from scratch, and your wins never make your ads any smarter.

The Hookline loop

One closed system: every lead tied to its exact ad, an AI brief and script for every call, recorded & transcribed conversations, and conversions fed back to Meta to make the next customer cheaper.

How it works

One loop, from first view to repeat winner.

Most agencies do step one and stop. Hookline runs the whole cycle, and AI works at every stage, so the system gets cheaper and smarter each time around.

Phase 1
Acquire
Get the click, capture the lead.
1
Creative goes live

We make or manage your Meta ads. Every ad is logged in your Ad Library under one consistent naming convention.

2
AI decodes every ad AI

It reads each ad frame-by-frame (1–2 frames/sec) and writes its hook, script, angle, pacing and writing pattern.

3
Lead captured & attributed

The viewer lands on your site and fills the form. The lead appears live in the dashboard, tagged to the exact ad that produced it.

Phase 2
Convert
Turn the lead into a customer.
4
A brief before every call AI

The rep opens the lead and instantly sees which ad it came from and exactly what that ad said, so they know what resonated.

5
AI writes the opening line AI

A tailored opening script, matched to the exact ad the person watched, so the call feels personal from hello.

6
Call, record, remember Exotel AI

Call in one click from the dashboard via Exotel. Every call is recorded & transcribed, and the next rep who picks up gets an AI summary of all prior conversations. No lead is ever worked cold twice.

Phase 3
Compound
Make the next customer cheaper.
7
Conversions feed Meta Meta CAPI

Mark a lead converted and the event fires straight back to Meta through the Conversions API, every win, automatically.

8
Optimise for real customers

Once you cross ~50 conversions a week, we switch Meta from chasing leads to optimising for actual conversions, cheaper buyers, not cheaper forms.

9
Winners & lookalikes AI

AI rewrites your winning ads into fresh scripts, and after ~1,000 buyers we build lookalike audiences from your real customers. Creative and targeting sharpen every month.

🔁 And the loop closes, every cycle cheaper, sharper and more automated than the last.
