The problem

Your agency is a black box, and its "AI" is a buzzword.

You wire the retainer and get a slide deck once a month. You can't see which creative is carrying the account, whether the leads were any good, or why one ad converts and another doesn't. And every agency now says "AI" without shipping a single thing you can actually use.

The opaque agency

Vanity metrics, monthly PDFs, no line of sight from ad spend to revenue, and creative made on gut feel. When a winner appears, nobody can say why it won, or make another one.

The Hookline way

A service plus a product. AI decodes every ad you run, ties each lead to its exact creative, and tells you the script behind your winners, then writes the next one.

The AI creative engine

A creative flywheel that compounds.

Most agencies guess at the next ad. We make it a system: every creative you run is read, scored, attributed and reproduced by AI, so each month you shoot more winners and fewer guesses.

1

Ad library

Every ad you run is uploaded to your library inside the platform, video and static, under one consistent naming convention.

2

AI decodes it

AI reads each ad frame-by-frame (1–2 frames/sec) and writes its hook, script, angle, pacing and even the writing pattern.

3

Attributed leads

We run the ads. Thanks to the naming convention, every lead lands tagged to the exact creative that produced it.

4

Winners surface

See which creatives drive conversions, and for subscription brands, which ones retain customers the longest.

5

AI rewrites the winner

The AI knows the winning script, so it generates fresh ones in that proven pattern. You shoot more winners, not guesses.

📈 Targeting that compounds too: every customer who fills your form flows into the platform. Once you cross ~1,000, we build lookalike audiences from your real buyers, so your targeting gets sharper every month, not just your creative.
